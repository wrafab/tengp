Final Project


1 & 2. In the last week, I designed a T-shirt with I love Hangzhou logo which was printed by the Brother GT-3 Series printer in the middle and I hand sewed a thick leather lag at the bottom.
For the tag I used Lazer cut to cut two holes so that I can sewed it on the T-shirt; I used UV printer to print picture of West Lake; Finally, I sprayed it to the black color.
3. I spent about 4hrs making this shirt.
4. Settings: Brother GT-3 Series: just uploaded the file and print. The logo was designed by the GTX graphics lab
UV print: I adjusted the position and uploaded the file and printed the logo. The image was designed by CoreDraw
Lazer cut machine: I set razer 90/60, and vector 50/100/50. The file was designed by CoreDraw
Hand sewing: I used the black string.
I learned that I should plan ahead. I was planing to design a bracelet. However, since I didn't get the length right, the leather ring and the 3-D printed ring didn't match
Then, I had to designed a shirt instead of the bracelet, which I finished the last night before presentation.
