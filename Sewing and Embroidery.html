Sewing and Embroidery


There are two parts of the project: 1. Hat 2. Pillowcase

In order to embroide the hat, I first need to set up my figure(what's on the hat). Then, I need to fix the hat by using a magnetic holder.
Finally, I just fix the holder onto the mechine, and using light to find the center and click start. The backing is for ending the process, avoiding string to left.
Here is the picture of my products.



The processes of makeing the pillowcase: 1. pick the color; 2. Cut the length that I want(3 types of length); 3. follow the instruction of embroiding; 4. Iron.
The challenge I met: I feel like the cutting is the hardest part. I cannot cut the edge of the cloth equally.
